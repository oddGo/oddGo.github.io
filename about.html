about us

In the beginning, there was a bright-eyed party of scrubs that entered the Ascalonian Catacombs. They were under-geared, and unprepared, but in their hearts shone a burning passion. They came to Guild Wars 2 from a different battlefield, seeking new ways to topple dudes and embark on glorious conquest. They forged a guild, dubbed the Get Fresh Crew, and they set out to make a name for themselves. Mercenaries, loyal only to each other and the hunger for heavy loot bags.

“Our mission is to provide WvW builds, guides, and gameplay advice to the members of GFC.”

GFC is a PvX guild at this stage in its life. But our common goal has remained much the same for however many shiney distractions we partake in; find fights, aquire bags. We play an MMORPG called Guild Wars 2, and the game mode we most focus on as a Guild is called World vs World. It is a large-scale conquest style match that lasts a week, where holding or capturing towers, keeps, and camps flows into hungrily seeking targets that have wronged our allies or perhaps just had the audacity to exist on the same map. We seek those fights, we break open those fortresses, and we wreck dudes!

Hardcore

This word is often tossed about without weight in the gaming sphere, but we are a hardcore guild. We play to win, and we push each other to play our best.

PPT-ShmeePT

It is the case that WvW's win condition is based on the Points-Per-Tick system of holding objectives to increase your score. Highest score at the end of the week might win, but we prefer the fights in between.

Revel

Like many groups, we record our footage for posterity. Many of our members have private Youtube content we share.
