Guardian

The guardian is an extremely durable class with the builds we run. It represents the defensive backbone for our group, providing condition cleanse, very powerful boon support, and can stat for damage or healing effectively.

Greatsword|Hammer / Staff Bruiser

Supportive Bruiser

Despite it's core function being support, this variant of our traditional Guardian opts for a secondary focus in damage. The stat block trades away it's healing power for damage stats so that after clearing an allies conditions, it can then club the target that put them their in the first place.

Hammer|Greatsword / Staff Support

Supportive Controller

This is the traditional healing power focused Guardian build. It is the condition clear, defensive boon, and healing support for our group runs, and it has reasonable cleave and strong CC.

Sceptor Shield / Staff Healer

Supportive Healer

The build is very similiar to the above support build, but focuses on the potential of Pure of Heart to output a great deal of burst healing as well. It focuses almost exclusively on this fucntion as its damage is milder than Support Hammer|Greatsword / Staff.
