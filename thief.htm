Thief

Thief is a highly mobile, evasive class with strong damage spike and very good chasing capability. Our thieves typically fetch targets for us through some potent initiation that has extensive range.

Sword Dagger Dare Acro

Evasive Chaser

This build for thief is designed to imitate marathon sprinters. It has huge chase potential, and mix of evades and damage. It's goal is to be one of the first into the fight to snare and help spike target.

Dagger Pistol Venom Interrupts

Interrupt Spiker

This build for thief is designed for locking targets down with fast, painful interrupts. Its shared venom is a nasty CC spike through grouped initiates on a given target.

Dagger Pistol Burst

Ganker

This is a non-Daredevil Thief filling the very traditional role of fast, hard damage spiker. It's played like a true ganker, singling out and quickly eliminating priority targets.
