Warrior

The modern warrior is typically an offensively-focused damage class. Our warrior builds bring high-melee cleave damage and cc, varying in levels with each builds focus.

Greatsword / Axe Shield Beserker

Build Link

This build's primary aim is to generate downs with heavy cc-into-damage combos. The build has high mobility and abuses the warrior's inherent durability and cooldowns to deliver it's strong cleave.

Hammer / Sword Warhorn Warrior

Build Link

This build's focuses on some of the support potential that warrior has. It's core aims are proving supportive cooldowns with warhorn and shouts, and cc with hammer and sword.
