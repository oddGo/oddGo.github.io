Mesmer

As a class, our Mesmers are regarded as very flexible blends of utility and damage. They help the group through heavy amplification of boon support. This is on top of some strong burst damage and control potential.

Sword Focus / Staff Boonshare Chrono

Boon Redoubler

The focus of this build is to vastly increase the value of our groups boons. Each boon produced by and given to the mes can be cycled back out to allies. It's a highly mobile build as well, and has strong damage and control with it's Shatter skills.
