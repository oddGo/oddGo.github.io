Engineer

Scrapper is a durable bruiser that focuses on cc and bringing a flexible amount of utility fucntions to group combat. It has respectable damage, but it's true strength lies in setting up targets for allies.

Hammer Scrapper

Build Link

Scrapper plays well with classes that benefit from it's strong initiations. Magnet pull is a staple opener for helping your damage-focused allies quickly snuff out a priorty target, and Thunderclap can provide an opening against multiple opponents. Scrapper has powerful revive potential through the function gyro, allowing it to rapidly revive a sinlge ally, or mutlti-task with reviving/stomping and using other skills.
