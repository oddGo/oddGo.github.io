Engineer

Engineer, through the Scrapper line, gained powerful personal sustain an stability access. The engineer with these new tools is far less likely to be CC/spike-comboed while he is firing off his own.

CC Power Scrapper

Pack Controller | Walnut Controller

Both the offensive Pack variant and the defensive Walnut variant server nearly the same function, with differently stat block focuses. The focus of the build is to setup strong CC chains during the initial clashes of fights, and followup with damage. The CC into damage combos are best when combined with allies. The strong regenerative capacity the build has allows Healing Turret to be used for allies more often.
